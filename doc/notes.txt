Practical authoring cheat sheet:

1. Add
==============================================
Use when you want:

    glow
    lamp bloom
    furnace light
    moonbeam lightening

Author as:

    colored bright center
    fade to transparent

Examples:

    orange → transparent
    blue → transparent
    pale yellow → transparent

2. Multiply
==============================================
    Use when you want:
    shadow
    dark falloff
    dimming band
    under-object darkness

Author as:

    white where there should be no change
    dark gray / black where there should be shadow

Examples:

    white edges, soft dark center
    white top, darker lower band
    white outside, dark soft oval inside
    You can still include alpha, but the main thing doing the shadowing is the RGB being darker than white.

3. Normal
==============================================
Use when you want:

    painted dark veil
    fog
    colored overlay
    subtle haze
    soft mask that should just visually sit on top

Author as:

    ordinary image with transparency
    black/colored translucent areas where effect exists
    transparent elsewhere

This is the most direct “artist says so” mode.
